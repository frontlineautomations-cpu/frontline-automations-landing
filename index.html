FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we get most.

Is this only for med spas?

No — med spas are our primary focus, but the system works for any appointment-based or service business.

Do I need to change my phone system?

Usually no. We can work with your existing provider or help you route calls properly.

How do you handle SMS consent?

We implement clear opt-in language and STOP/HELP handling. Your workflows should only message opted-in contacts.

What if the AI can’t answer something?

It can route to a human, take a message, or send a booking link — depending on the scenario you choose.
